Weapons and Abilities

There are many weapons and abilities in the Stars Wars Universe, so I will just go over the most used. The chosen weapons for stormtroopers are blasters. Most simple way of picturing the gun is thinking of guns that shoots out lasers. The weapon of choice for the Jedi/Sith were lightsabers. They are also know as laser swords. The Sith/Jedi had special abilities, some better than others, and it was called the Force. The Force in the Star Wars universe is the energy of life itself. The Jedi/Sith could harness this energy and move objects,trick weak minds, seeing the future, and much more.
